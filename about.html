OUR STORY

A legacy of culinary innovation and the pursuit of perfect fusion.

01 OUR GENESIS

Cornerstone inherits the ethnic cooking skills of modern Chinese cuisine, featuring Peking Duck, Xiao Long Bao (soup dumpling), Grill Fish and etc. as the signature dishes. We serve not only indulgent and comforting dining experiences featuring the best of Chinese fusion flavors from each region of China, but also consistently high-quality food and ingredients and superior customer service.

With a practical attitude, diligent spirit, and innovative mindset, Cornerstone aims to achieve the strategic goal of expanding to 40 stores across the East Coast, West Coast, and Florida over the next three years and offer every diner the unique experience of refined banquets and gatherings through our Chinese fusion.

Cornerstone

02 THE JOURNEY

Our first location was established in Flushing, NY, the heart of an Asian community neighborhood. Running a restaurant in Flushing can be very intimidating, but we made it with a strong mission within our team, which is to inherit the beauty of ethnic Chinese Cuisine. In the future, we will be opening more stores.

03 OUR PHILOSOPHY

To offer an indulgent and comforting dining experience featuring the best of Chinese fusion flavors and ethical cooking practices, including traditional entrees, seasonal menus with trending ingredients, and creative drink menus. We prioritize consistently high-quality food and ingredients, superior customer service, and convenience for banquets and gatherings.
