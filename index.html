Our Story

Rooted in tradition. Designed for today.

Inspired by the Jiangnan region of China, our kitchen blends time-honored recipes with a contemporary sensibility. Every dish is crafted to balance texture, aroma, and flavor, paired with a curated list of wines, teas, and cocktails.

From the lighting to the tableware, every detail is designed to create a warm, intimate atmosphere—perfect for celebrations, date nights, and unforgettable evenings with friends.
